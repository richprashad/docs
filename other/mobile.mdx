## Overview

Flow is a Progressive Web App (PWA), meaning you can bookmark it on your mobile device, allowing you to access it quickly with an and icon, just like a native mobile app.Â 

Flow is mobile-responsive, meaning that the layout and UI are optimized for mobile devices.

It supports all actions you can take on the desktop, except for chat. Chat will be available on mobile at the end of Q4 2025, once our upgrade to the Chat UI and intelligence is complete.

Here are instructions on how to [bookmark Flow to your iOS device](https://discussions.apple.com/thread/255899735?sortBy=rank).
